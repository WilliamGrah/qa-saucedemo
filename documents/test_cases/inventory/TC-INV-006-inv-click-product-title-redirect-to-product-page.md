# Test Case: TC-INV-006 - Click Product Title Redirects to Product Page

## Test Information
- **Test Suite:** Inventory
- **Test Environment:** Production (Edge 143.0.3650.96, Windows 11 Pro)
- **Status:** Passed

---

## Preconditions
- User logged in with valid credentials (standard_user/secret_sauce)
- Browser is open and on the products page

---

## Steps
1. Navigate to https://www.saucedemo.com/inventory.html
2. Click on the title of any product (e.g., Sauce Labs Backpack)
3. It should redirect to the product detail page for that specific product

---

## Expected Result
- User is on the product detail page for the selected product
- The page displays the product's name, description, price, and image

---

## Actual Result
- Behaved as expected
